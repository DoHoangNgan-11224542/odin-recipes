Bun Bo Hue

Description

Bun Bo Hue is a popular Vietnamese soup containing rice vermicelli and beef. Originating from the city of Hue in central Vietnam, it is greatly admired for its balance of spicy, sour, salty, and sweet flavors and the predominant flavor of lemongrass.

Ingredients:

  • Thick rice noodles
  • Beef bones and pork knuckles
  • Lemongrass and shrimp paste
  • Chili oil
  • Sliced beef brisket

Steps:

  1. Simmer beef bones and pork knuckles with crushed lemongrass to make the broth.
  2. Season the broth with fermented shrimp paste and chili oil for the signature color and spicy taste.
  3. Boil the thick rice noodles until soft.
  4. Place noodles and meat in a bowl, pour the boiling hot broth over them, and serve with fresh vegetables.
