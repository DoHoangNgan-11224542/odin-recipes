Bun Cha

Description

Bun Cha is a famous and iconic dish from Hanoi. It features perfectly grilled pork served with rice noodles, fresh herbs, and a signature sweet and sour dipping sauce. It became globally famous when President Obama ate it in Hanoi!

Ingredients:

  • Rice noodles (Bun)
  • Pork belly and minced pork
  • Fish sauce, sugar, and vinegar
  • Green papaya and carrots
  • Fresh herbs

Steps:

  1. Marinate the minced pork and pork belly with spices.
  2. Grill the pork over hot charcoal until nicely browned.
  3. Make the dipping broth by mixing fish sauce, water, vinegar, and sugar.
  4. Put the grilled pork into the broth bowl, serve with noodles and fresh herbs on the side.
