Pho

Description

Pho is a traditional cuisine in Vietnam. You must try it when you come to Vietnam, especially Hanoi because in each region there are different type of Pho.

Ingredients:

  • Pho noodles
  • Pho broth
  • Beef

Steps:

  1. Put beef into broth.
  2. Boil it until the beef is cooked.
  3. Pour the broth with beef into a bowl of noodles, and enjoy!
